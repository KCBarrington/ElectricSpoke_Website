The Vision

Urban mobility, elevated.

ElectricSpoke was born from the belief that how we move through a city should feel as considered as any other act of modern living. Sebastian is the expression of that belief — a machine designed not merely to transport, but to delight.

Every proportion, every surface, every connection point has been interrogated. The result is a scooter that belongs in the company of objects made to be appreciated for a lifetime.
