Craftsmanship & Components

Built from exceptional materials and proven components.

Sebastian combines precision-engineered structure with carefully selected premium components drawn from the worlds of automotive, BMX and mountain bike design. Every material and component has been chosen not simply for performance, but for tactile quality, durability and character.

Designed in the UK and developed with specialist British manufacturing partners, Sebastian brings together precision engineering, traditional craft and carefully selected performance components.

Featuring components from: Magura Lupine Samsung VESC Alienation Vans

The Vision

Urban mobility, elevated.

ElectricSpoke was born from the belief that how we move through a city should feel as considered as any other act of modern living. Sebastian is the expression of that belief — a machine designed not merely to transport, but to delight.

Every proportion, every surface, every connection point has been interrogated. The result is a scooter that belongs in the company of objects made to be appreciated for a lifetime.

"Sebastian is the first e-scooter I've encountered that one might describe as genuinely beautiful."

— ElectricSpoke Design Studio
